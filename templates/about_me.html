About Me

So, who am I?

My name is Johan, and I’m passionate about fishing. With over 12 years of experience, I’ve spent countless hours out on lakes and along the ocean, perfecting my skills and connecting with nature. Fishing isn’t just a hobby for me—it’s a way of life. I enjoy the thrill of the catch and the tranquility that comes with being out on the water. My favorite targets are Perch, Pike, and Zander, and I’ve developed a real knack for understanding their habits and finding the best spots to reel them in. Whether it’s the stillness of a lake or the rolling waves of the ocean, fishing brings me a sense of freedom and excitement that I can’t find anywhere else. Let’s connect and share our fishing experiences, tips, and love for the water!
